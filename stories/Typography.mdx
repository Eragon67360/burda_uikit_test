## Typography Variants

### Responsive Typography Classes

All typography classes are responsive and adapt between mobile and desktop:

- `.text-h1` to `.text-h6`
- `.text-subhead1` to `.text-subhead4`
- `.text-copy`
- `.text-copy-small`
- And many more...

### Special Typography Classes

- Subscription: `.text-subscription-default`, `.text-subscription-small`
- Hotline Footer: `.text-hotline-footer-default`, `.text-hotline-footer-small`
- Order Step: `.text-order-step`
- Button Labels: `.text-button-label`, `.text-button-label-large`
- Links: `.text-link`, `.text-link-small`
